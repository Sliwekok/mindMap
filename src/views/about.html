About app

  • This app allows you to create mind map with data and connect it.
  • You can choose 4 size of map - based on selected value the map will grow or shrink
  • To zoom in or out you hold CTRL key and scrollmouse wheel
  • To scroll and change camera position you holdCTRL key and holdleft click on empty map space and move it to some direction
  • You can add relation by clicking on switch button (upper-left corner of card) and navigating to meant-to card.
  • Remember about adding name to your mind up (upper-left in nav) and click save whenever you're ready to go!

Data

App uses local storage to save files and to load them dynamically. You can delete all your data but keep in mind already stored files will stay on your machine.
